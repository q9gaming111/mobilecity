Features

Samsung Galaxy A12 (6.5'', 128GB/4GB, 5000 mAh) - Black

6.5” Infinity-V Display

Get drawn into the action with the 6.5” Infinity-V Display and see what you've been missing. Thanks to the immersive 20:9 display ratio, you’ll see the whole scene on the screen.

Upgrade your mobile photography

Snap your memorable moments in clear detail with the 48MP Main Camera. Level up your photography with the intelligent Ultra Wide Camera, new Macro Camera and refined Depth Camera.

Power that keeps you going

Stay ahead with a battery that won't slow you down. The Galaxy A12’s long-lasting 5,000mAh (typical) battery quickly reaches full power with 15W Adaptive Fast Charging.

Enjoy more room to play

Download more and delete less with up to 128GB internal storage, giving you quick access to your favourite content, files and photos. Add more storage with a 512GB microSD card.

Protect what matters to you

Built into the phone's hardware and software from the start, Samsung Knox is designed to protect your phone with multi-layered security. Side Fingerprint access means only you can unlock your Galaxy A12.

Attention-grabbing selfies

It’s your time to shine. The Galaxy A12 8MP Selfie Camera with Smart Beauty filter makes it easy to snap stunning selfies that feature more you and less background.

Made to focus with One UI Core

One UI Core helps you focus on what’s important. Hardware and software work together to keep content and features at your fingertips. You’ll also enjoy the option of Dark Mode.

Ultra-performance

Galaxy A12 combines the processing power of Octa Core Chipset processing power with up to 4GB of RAM for fast and efficient performance. Best of all, 128GB storage means you can keep more on your phone.
