Help & Support

Payment

PayPal

  • In order to process your orders without any delays, we strongly recommend you to pay using a PayPal Verified account

Credit Cards (Visa/Mastercard)

  • Your credit card payment is processed our by eWay via a secure SSL 256-bit encryption technology. Your credit card information entered into our website is encrypted and will be sent by eWay directly to the bank to be processed. Therefore, we do not keep your credit card and financial information.
  • 3D Secure will be applied as an additional security measure for fraud-protection. This is a tool which requests an internet banking PIN before processing each transaction. Customers with 3D Secure activated on their credit card will be redirected to a webpage hosted by their bank and asked to enter a PIN, verifying their identity. This pin can be sent to you in a variety of ways, most commonly by SMS. For more information please contact your financial institution.

Zip

  • Zip offers a convenient buy now, pay later, interest free payment solution
  • You can apply for a Zip account here
  • To use your account, simply select Zip at checkout.

Direct Deposit

  • Our Westpac bank account details are shown as below:
    1. Account Name: Mobileciti
    2. BSB No: 032277
    3. Account No: 254384
    4. Reference: Your order number
  • When you are making the payment please use your order number as reference to avoid any delay in processing your purchase. Please note it may take more than 24 hours to process the bank transfer.

Pay in Store

We accept cash, EFTPOS, Debit Card, Visa, Mastercard, AMEX (3.3% surcharge on AMEX) and Zip in our Lidcombe store.
